Youth Leadership, Entrepreneurship & Empowerment Program (Youth LEEP)

Young Leaders Network is equipping youth with essential skills in leadership and entrepreneurship. Through training and resources, YLN is raising a new generation of youth who can drive economic growth, particularly in underserved areas.

Soft Skills Training

In partnership with Jobberman Nigeria, YLN is mobilizing and training youth aged 18–35 in soft and employability skills. This initiative empowers youth with essential tools to secure dignified jobs and succeed in the modern workforce.

ILEAD School of Leadership and Civic Engagement

Through the ILEAD School, YLN develops young graduates into civic-minded leaders equipped with leadership, work-readiness, and social change skills. The program targets individuals passionate about community impact and personal growth.

Young Leaders Development Program (YLDP)

The YLDP is a transformative program that equips students with leadership, life, and entrepreneurial skills. It inspires value-based leadership and empowers youth to advocate for themselves and their communities.

Ignite Talk

Ignite Talk is a monthly platform for youth to engage in intellectual discussions, mentorship, and professional development. It encourages a culture of growth, resilience, and active learning among young changemakers.

Ignite Sports

Ignite Sports promotes youth wellness through sports events, fitness challenges, and wellness workshops. It has inspired over 400 participants to embrace health, teamwork, and personal growth.

YLN

Young Leaders Network is a youth-led nonprofit empowering Africa’s next generation through leadership, education, and social impact.
